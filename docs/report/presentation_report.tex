\title{COSC 520 Presentation: Recommender Algorithms}

\begin{abstract}
This report examines modern recommender systems with a focus on their historical development, core algorithmic structure, and societal implications. We analyze issues related to privacy, fairness, psychological well-being, and ideological escalation. Finally, we discuss potential technical and ethical solutions that align recommender systems with user well-being and platform accountability.
\end{abstract}
